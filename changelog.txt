Version: 2.0.0
Date: 2025-12-20
  Info:
    - First 2.0 release of Drill of Drills.
  Major Features:
    - Drills of Drills now appear on the tech tree as their own separate techs.
    - Drills of Drills now scale in odd increments, ensuring that pipe connections and outputs remain centered.
  Locale:
    - Fixed broken locale keys on recipes.
  Balancing:
    - Drills of Drills can now mine much faster. 
    - By default, they are capped at half of the fastest belt's throughput.
    - If the drill can output stacked items, the cap is adjusted for a fully stacked belt.
    - Drills that output fluids are limited to maximum fluid throughput for 1 connection.
    - Drills that snap to deposits exactly (like pumpjacks) are still excluded from Drills of Drills.
    - Productivity is not factored into the speed cap.
    - Drills of Drills now have a configurable max size. (default 32x32 tiles)

Version: 1.0.0
Date: 2023-10-12
  Info:
    - First release of Drill of Drills.
  Major Features:
    - Drills of drills for each drill.
      Drills with mining areas smaller than their footprint are excluded.
      Drills that mine more than 60 outputs per second are excluded and will not be created.
    - Drills of drills unlock when their normal counterpart does.
    - Drills of drills are made from an equivalent square area of their normal counterpart.
    - Drills of drills can be upgraded into larger versions or disassembled into their normal counterparts with zero loss.
